# Supplementary Derivations for “Fault-Aware Design for Reconfigurable Holographic Surface-Aided ISAC Systems”

A supplementary document is provided with the detailed derivations of A_η0 and B_η0 used for the paper L. Wang, M. Delbari, G. Zhou, L. F. Abanto-Leon, M. Hollick, and V. Jamali, “Fault-Aware Design for Reconfigurable Holographic Surface-Aided ISAC Systems,” _2026 IEEE International Symposium on Personal, Indoor and Mobile Radio Communications (PIMRC)_, 2026. These derivations are omitted from the main paper due to space limitations.

The equation numbering in the supplementary document follows the numbering sequence of the main paper.

You can also find the paper from here: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.03013
